Broadband Internet and Fiber Optic Expansion Program

Building a Connected Community

At Strategic Alliance Community Development Corporation, our mission is to strengthen and empower the community of Detroit through our Broadband Internet and Fiber Optic Expansion Program. We understand the critical importance of reliable, high-speed internet in fostering a modern, thriving community.

Transforming Digital Infrastructure

Our program focuses on the development and expansion of fiber optic networks across the city, bridging the digital divide and improving the quality of life for all residents. Working hand-in-hand with the city of Detroit, we are not just installing cables; we are reshaping the future of our city.

A Future Connected

We believe every resident deserves access to high-speed internet. Our commitment extends beyond simply addressing today's needs; we aim to ensure a connected community for generations to come. By partnering with us in this mission, you are investing in the lasting growth and resilience of the Detroit community.

Join Us

Together, we can bridge the digital divide. Together, we can build a more connected, empowered Detroit.
